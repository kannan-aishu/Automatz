Water & wastewater

The Automation and control systems are working at the various processes of the Waste water industry. The Process of Wastewater treatment is Electro coagulation, Dosing, Ultra Filtration and Decantry.

Solution

Ausweg Automation offers a wide range of control & automation solutions for the Water & Waste water Industry. Our automation solutions consist of sensors, PLC, VFD, AC Servo Drive & Motors, HMI and software package that support a wide range of Automation and process control solutions effectively meet the monitoring and control requirements of water treatment with increased plant safety and availability.

Benifits

  • Optimize energy efficiency.
  • Avoid water loss.
  • Reduce combination of water bodies.
  • Reduced need for chemical Additives.
  • Increasing treatment capacity.
