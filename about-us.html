About Us

Home About Us

We're a company built on a clear set of values. Our goal is to empower you to do your best work in the most efficient way possible. Ausweg Automation integrates Operational Technology (OT) and Information Technology (IT) to provide innovative and customized solutions for sustainable future. We Conceptualize, Plan, Design, Build and Deploy high quality, engineering solutions in the field of mechatronics, industrial automation, robotics & IIoT.

OT engineers and industrial control system operators may better manage essential systems and assets across the plant / factory with our user-friendly IT platforms. Collaborate as a team on a single platform, share data across groups, and solve problems faster. Our mission is to improve the quality of life by making the world more productive and sustainable by the Technology.
